About Shubham Trading

A strong nationwide B2B supply network built on reliability and authentic regional sourcing.

Two Decades of Excellence

Established in 2004, Shubham Trading Company has grown into a premier B2B bulk supplier operating out of Kolkata, India. We specialize in eliminating intermediaries to procure authentic regional Indian ingredients straight from the farmers and origin points.

Our robust pan-India logistics network reliably serves a vast institutional clientele including top-tier Hotels,Hospitals, Restaurants, Caterers, Defense (Army/BSF) canteens, and wholesale distributors. We pride ourselves on consistent quality, volume reliability, and transparent trade practices.
