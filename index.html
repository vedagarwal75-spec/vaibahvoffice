Our Story

Two Decades of Authentic Regional Sourcing

Founded in 2004 in Kolkata, Shubham Trading Company began with a single mission: to connect India's diverse regional agricultural bounty with the institutional buyers who need it most — hotels, restaurants, catering companies, hospitals, defence canteens and wholesale distributors.

Over two decades, we have built what we consider one of the most comprehensive direct-sourcing networks in the country. From the Basmati-growing belts of Haryana to the spice estates of Kerala, from Rajasthan's cumin farms to the aromatic rice paddies of Bengal — our procurement partnerships span 18+ states and hundreds of individual origin points.

"We don't just supply ingredients — we supply authenticity. Every product in our catalogue is traceable to its origin state, ensuring our buyers receive the genuine variety their kitchens and customers demand."

— Shubham Trading Company, Kolkata

Our client base today includes five-star hotel chains, large restaurant groups, institutional catering companies, army and BSF canteen supply chains, regional wholesale distributors, and food processors across India. Each relationship is built on consistent supply, competitive pricing and the reliability that comes with 20+ years of operating experience.
